    6. Troubleshooting & FAQs

    Why aren't popups opening?
    Most likely your browser blocked them. Allow pop-ups for the site and try again. (Static disclaimer at the advanced panel explains this.)
    Why do some pings fail?
    CORS policies and endpoint rate limits vary. Ping mode depends on server willingness to accept cross-origin requests.
    Why are results slow on mobile?
    Mobile devices have less CPU/RAM and may throttle multiple concurrent operations. Reduce concurrency and use IFrame mode for best stability.
    Is it safe to use auto submission?
    Yes ‚Äî the tool runs client-side. However, respect external platforms' terms and avoid spammy volume that violates service rules.
