    Your browser is likely blocking pop-ups. Look for a pop-up blocker icon in your address bar and allow pop-ups for this site. Alternatively, use IFrame mode which doesn't require pop-up permissions.

    Some submissions show as failures. Is this normal?

    Yes, some template services may be temporarily unavailable, rate-limited, or blocked by your network. The tool is designed to continue despite individual failures.

    The progress seems slow. How can I speed it up?

    Increase the concurrency setting in Advanced options, but be aware that very high values may trigger security measures or overwhelm your system.

    Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

    Yes, but Popup/Tab modes may be less reliable on mobile browsers. IFrame mode typically works best on mobile platforms.

    How often should I run this backlink generator?

    For optimal results, run it daily for new content and weekly for established pages. Combine with other promotion methods for comprehensive coverage.
