    Solution: Your browser is blocking pop-ups. Look for the pop-up blocker icon in your address bar and allow pop-ups for this site. Alternatively, use IFrame mode as a backup.

    CORS & Rate Limiting

    Problem: Some submissions show as failures due to CORS errors.
    Solution: This is normal behavior for certain services. The tool includes multiple backup services, so occasional CORS failures don't significantly impact overall results.

    Problem: Tool runs slowly or browser becomes unresponsive.
    Solution: Reduce concurrency settings, close other browser tabs, or switch to Ping mode for faster operation with reduced resource usage.
