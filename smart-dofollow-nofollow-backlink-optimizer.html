    Troubleshooting & Frequently Asked Questions

    Popup Windows Not Opening

    Problem: No windows open when using Popup/Tab mode.
    Solution: Your browser is blocking pop-ups. Look for the pop-up blocker icon in your address bar and click it, then select "Always allow pop-ups from this site." Alternatively, use IFrame mode which doesn't require pop-up permissions.

    Slow Performance or Browser Freezing

    Problem: Browser becomes unresponsive during submission.
    Solution: Reduce the concurrency setting to 3-5 windows. Close other browser tabs and applications to free up system resources. Consider using Ping mode for faster completion on limited hardware.
